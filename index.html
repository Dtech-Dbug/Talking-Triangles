What is a Triangle?

A triangle is a polygon with three edges and three vertices. It is one of the basic shapes in geometry

    More Informations on triangles.
  • No. of vertices : 3
  • No. of Edges: 3
  • Area: ½ × base × height
  • Perimeter: Sum of three sides
  • Sum of interior angles: 180degrees
